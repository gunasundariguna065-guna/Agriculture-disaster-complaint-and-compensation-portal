🌾 Agricultural Disaster Complaint and Compensation Portal

Project Description

The Agricultural Disaster Complaint and Compensation Portal is an AI-powered web application that helps farmers report crop damage caused by natural disasters such as floods, heavy rainfall, droughts, cyclones, pest attacks, and crop diseases. The system enables government officers to verify complaints, recommend suitable government schemes, approve compensation requests, and provide timely support. Farmers can track the status of their complaints in real time, making the compensation process faster, more transparent, and more efficient.

- Backend: Python (Flask/Django)
- Database: MySQL
- AI/ML: TensorFlow, OpenCV, Scikit-learn

Users

- Farmers
- Government Officers
- Administrators
- Agricultural Experts

Project Workflow

1. Farmer registers and logs in.
2. Farmer submits a crop damage complaint with images and location.
3. AI analyzes the uploaded data and estimates the damage.
4. Government officers review the complaint.
5. The complaint is approved or rejected.
6. Eligible farmers receive compensation and government scheme recommendations.
7. Farmers can track the complaint status until completion.
